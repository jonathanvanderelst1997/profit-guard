# App Store listing draft

## Name
Profit Guard

## Tagline
Find products that are quietly killing your profit.

## Description
Profit Guard scans your Shopify products and flags variants with negative margin, low margin, or missing cost data before pricing mistakes hurt your store.

## Features
- Margin scan by variant.
- Minimum margin target.
- Loss detection.
- Missing cost detection.
- Supplier CSV cost preview.
- Findings CSV export.
- Optional weekly alert email.

## Disclaimer
Profit Guard calculates gross product margin from selling price and product cost available in Shopify. It does not guarantee net profit.
