# Outreach pack

Target stores: 100+ SKUs, physical products, supplier cost changes, wholesale/B2B, frequent CSV/bulk edits.

## Email
Subject: Quick Shopify margin check?

Hi {{first_name}},

I’m building Profit Guard, a tiny Shopify app that scans your products and flags items sold at a loss, below target margin, or missing cost data.

It is read-only. No price changes.

Would a quick margin-risk scan be useful after supplier cost changes or bulk edits?

## Qualification questions
1. How many SKUs do you have?
2. Do you store product cost in Shopify?
3. How often do supplier costs change?
4. Have bulk edits caused pricing mistakes?
5. Would you pay $19-$49/month if this caught mistakes?
