# Outreach pack

Target stores: 100+ SKUs, physical products, supplier cost changes, wholesale/B2B, frequent CSV/bulk edits.

## Cold email

Subject: quick Shopify margin leak check?

Hi {{first_name}},

I’m building Margin Sentinel, a Shopify app that scans your catalog for products with missing costs, negative margin, prices below your target margin, or inventory value at risk.

The useful bit: it turns the scan into a short fix list with the margin gap, inventory dollars at risk, suggested minimum price, and a supplier-cost what-if check. It is read-only and never changes prices automatically.

Would a quick margin leak scan be useful after supplier cost changes, discounts, or bulk catalog edits?

Thanks,
Jonathan

## Qualification questions
1. How many SKUs do you have?
2. Do you store product cost in Shopify?
3. How often do supplier costs change?
4. Have bulk edits caused pricing mistakes?
5. Who fixes pricing or cost mistakes today?
6. Would a supplier-cost what-if before price updates or promos be useful?
7. Would you pay $15-$39/month if this caught mistakes before they reached orders?

## Best first prospects

- Shopify stores with 500+ physical SKUs.
- Apparel, accessories, parts, home goods, beauty, supplements, hobby supplies.
- Stores that import supplier price lists or edit products in bulk.
- Wholesale/B2B stores with negotiated supplier costs.
- Agencies managing catalog operations for Shopify clients.

## Demo talk track

1. Import supplier CSV or use Shopify unit costs.
2. Set target gross margin.
3. Run scan.
4. Open Action Center and fix the first product.
5. Show inventory dollars at risk.
6. Run a cost-change what-if scenario.
7. Export the full fix list.
8. Enable weekly alerts.
