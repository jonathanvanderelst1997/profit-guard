# Outreach pack

Target stores: 100+ SKUs, physical products, supplier cost changes, wholesale/B2B, frequent CSV/bulk edits.

## Qualification questions
1. How many SKUs do you have?
2. Do you store product cost in Shopify?
3. How often do supplier costs change?
4. Have bulk edits caused pricing mistakes?
5. Who fixes pricing or cost mistakes today?
6. Would you pay $15-$39/month if this caught mistakes before they reached orders?

## Best first prospects

- Shopify stores with 500+ physical SKUs.
- Apparel, accessories, parts, home goods, beauty, supplements, hobby supplies.
- Stores that import supplier price lists or edit products in bulk.
- Wholesale/B2B stores with negotiated supplier costs.
- Agencies managing catalog operations for Shopify clients.
