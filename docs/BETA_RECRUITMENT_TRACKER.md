# Beta recruitment tracker

Goal: get 10 Shopify merchants or agencies to run one Margin Sentinel scan.

## Success criteria

Count a beta as successful when the merchant:

1. Installs the app or joins a guided screen share.
2. Runs one scan.
3. Imports supplier costs or confirms Shopify unit costs are present.
4. Reviews the Action Center.
5. Runs or reviews a supplier-cost what-if scenario.
6. Answers: "Did this find something useful you would otherwise have missed?"
