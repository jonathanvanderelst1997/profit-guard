# Risk register

1. Merchants may not store costs in Shopify. Mitigation: missing-cost warnings + CSV preview.
2. Large stores exceed simple pagination. Mitigation: V2 bulk operations.
3. Margin may differ from accounting due to fees/shipping/tax. Mitigation: call it gross product margin, no profit guarantee.
4. App Store review delay. Mitigation: minimal scopes, clear privacy, uninstall data deletion.
5. Existing apps copy feature. Mitigation: simple UX, strong positioning, early reviews.
