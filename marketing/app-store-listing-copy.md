# Margin Sentinel App Store listing copy

## Positioning

Do not sell Margin Sentinel as another profit dashboard. Sell it as a SKU-level margin leak scanner that gives merchants an exception list.

## Primary headline

Find missing costs, low-margin SKUs, and margin leaks before they eat profit.

## Short description

Margin Sentinel scans your Shopify catalog for missing COGS, low-margin SKUs, supplier cost gaps, inventory risk, and what-if margin scenarios. It gives merchants a prioritized exception list without changing prices automatically.

## Keyword targets

Shopify profit tracker, COGS tracker, margin calculator, SKU profit, product margin, missing costs, discount margin, supplier cost, inventory risk, Shopify margin, low-margin products, product cost tracking.

## Long description

Revenue is easy to see. Margin leaks are quieter.

Margin Sentinel helps Shopify merchants find product-level margin problems before those issues turn into expensive mistakes.

Run a SKU-level scan to spot:

- Missing COGS or unit costs
- Low-margin SKUs
- Products selling below target margin
- Supplier cost gaps
- Inventory tied up in risky products
- What-if margin impact from supplier cost increases

Margin Sentinel is built as an exception list, not another generic dashboard. The goal is to tell merchants which products need attention today.

The app reads Shopify product and inventory data, Shopify unit costs, and merchant-imported supplier costs. It stays read-only and does not automatically change prices, inventory, themes, checkout, or customer data.

Use Margin Sentinel when:

- Supplier costs live in spreadsheets
- Shopify unit costs are missing or stale
- Discounts may be eating margin
- A catalog has many variants
- The team needs a CSV fix list
- Merchants want a weekly margin leak email

Best first aha:

We found 12 variants with missing costs.

That first result gives merchants a clear next step: add costs, import supplier data, review low-margin SKUs, export the fix list, or run a what-if margin scenario.

## Review-safe claims

- Read-only catalog margin scanner.
- Does not automatically change prices.
- Does not claim full net-profit accounting.
- Does not include ad spend, refunds, shipping, or order-level profit unless future order permissions and integrations are added.
