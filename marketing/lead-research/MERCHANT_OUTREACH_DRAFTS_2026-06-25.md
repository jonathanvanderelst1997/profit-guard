# Merchant outreach drafts - 2026-06-25

These are direct-merchant drafts based on public catalog signals.

Do not send without exact approval. Prefer 1-3 highly specific messages first, not a large batch.

## Why these merchants

The public probe found Shopify stores where the publicly exposed product catalog suggests enough product/variant complexity to make SKU-level margin checks relevant.

Use this as a signal, not as a claim that their costs are wrong.

Candidate:

- BulbAmerica
- Public signal: at least 2,000 products counted before the probe cap.
- Angle: commodity lighting catalog, supplier price changes, margin pressure.

Subject:

```text
Quick Shopify catalog margin question
```

Body:

```text
Hi BulbAmerica team,

I was looking at Shopify stores with large product catalogs and noticed your lighting catalog has a lot of SKU depth.

I built Margin Sentinel, a read-only Shopify app for finding catalog margin issues before they become expensive:

- products selling below cost
- products below target margin
- missing Shopify unit cost or supplier cost data
- inventory risk tied to margin gaps
- CSV export for the fix list

It does not change prices automatically.

For supplier-heavy catalogs, the use case is usually: supplier costs change, prices do not always move at the same time, and the team needs to know which SKUs need review first.

Would it be worth sending you a short demo, or is margin/cost review already handled well internally?

App Store listing:
https://apps.shopify.com/margin-sentinel

Best,
Jonathan
```
