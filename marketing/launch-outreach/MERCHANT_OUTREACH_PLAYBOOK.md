# Merchant outreach playbook

## Lead filters
Prioritize stores that likely have:
- 100+ products or many variants.
- Physical inventory.
- Frequent supplier/import/catalog changes.
- Promotions or markdowns.
- Categories where margin varies by SKU.

Best niches:
- apparel and accessories
- beauty and skincare
- outdoor gear
- electronics accessories
- auto/bike/machine parts
- home goods
- pet products
- hobby/craft supplies
- gifts and stationery

Avoid:
- digital products only
- single-product stores
- marketplaces with unclear ownership
- medical/regulated claims-heavy categories

## Research fields
- Store name
- Website
- Contact email or form URL
- Niche
- Estimated catalog complexity
- Why Margin Sentinel is relevant
- First sentence personalization
- Status

## Merchant email 1
Subject: Quick Shopify margin check for {store}

Hi {team/name},

I noticed {personal_detail}. I just launched Margin Sentinel on the Shopify App Store. It helps merchants with physical catalogs find SKU-level margin leaks before they disappear inside broad reports.

It checks Shopify prices, unit costs, and supplier CSV costs, then flags:
- products losing money
- variants below your margin target
- missing cost data
- inventory value exposed to margin gaps
- suggested minimum margin guidance

The app is read-only and does not change prices.

Listing:
https://apps.shopify.com/margin-sentinel

Would you be open to running one scan and telling me if the findings are useful?

Best,
Jonathan

P.S. If this is not useful, reply "no thanks" and I will not follow up.
