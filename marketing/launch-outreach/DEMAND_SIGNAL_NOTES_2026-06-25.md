# Demand signal notes - 2026-06-25

These are public pain signals to guide content and community replies.

## Strongest problem themes

1. Supplier CSV price updates are manual and SKU matching is painful.
   - Shopify Community: `https://community.shopify.com/t/how-can-i-efficiently-update-prices-using-a-csv-file/138448`

2. Merchants want cost, price, and margin visibility while receiving inventory.
   - Shopify Community: `https://community.shopify.com/t/feature-request-add-cost-price-margin-display-to-purchase-order-receiving/587455`

3. Variant-level margin visibility is confusing or incomplete for some merchants.
   - Shopify Community: `https://community.shopify.com/t/product-profit-margin-does-not-show-on-variants/303113`

4. Merchants need reports for products missing cost of goods / cost per item.
   - Shopify Community: `https://community.shopify.com/t/pull-report-of-products-that-do-not-have-cogs/90212`

5. Merchants still rely on spreadsheets for supplier costs, shipping, currency, retail price, net profit, and margin percentage.
   - Reddit result: `https://www.reddit.com/r/shopify/comments/1ajwpe5/collections_and_main_menus/`

6. Gross profit reports can fail or become misleading when cost per item is missing.
   - Shopify Community: `https://community.shopify.com/t/why-are-recent-sales-not-appearing-in-gross-profit/175762`

## Positioning implication

Margin Sentinel should not try to sound like a full profit analytics platform yet.

Best narrow promise:

`A read-only Shopify scan that finds missing costs, loss-making variants, and low-margin catalog risk before supplier updates or price changes become expensive.`

## Best content angles this week

- "Before you import a supplier CSV, check which SKUs would fall below margin."
- "How to find Shopify products missing cost per item."
- "Why variant-level margin checks matter in large catalogs."
- "A safer workflow for bulk price updates: scan, review, export, then change prices."
- "Read-only margin checks for teams that do not want an app changing prices automatically."
