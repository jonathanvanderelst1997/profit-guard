# Shopify App Store optimization plan

## Immediate issue
The app is currently published with limited visibility. If the goal is organic discovery, change visibility to full visibility in Shopify Partner Dashboard after confirming the live listing, support email, and billing are stable.

## Listing URL

- profit margin

- pricing

Use these naturally. Do not keyword-stuff.

## Current angle
"Catalog margin scanner that finds margin leaks across variants."

## Suggested app details improvement
Margin Sentinel scans Shopify variants for missing costs, low margins, and loss-making products. Import supplier costs by CSV, quantify inventory risk, and export a prioritized fix list with suggested minimum margin guidance. The app is read-only and never changes prices automatically.

## Suggested feature bullets
- Scan variants for missing costs, loss-making products, and low margins.
- Import supplier costs by CSV and preview catalog impact.
- Quantify inventory risk from margin gaps and export prioritized fixes.

## Screenshot strategy
1. Dashboard: issues found, inventory risk, suggested minimum price.
2. Supplier import: CSV import, import history, updated variants.
3. What-if: supplier cost increase impact and margin risk.
4. Export/actions: downloadable fix list and next action guidance.

## Listing conversion checklist
- App Store URL works.
- Pricing is clear.
- Support link works.
- Privacy/terms/refund links work.
- Screenshots contain no disallowed pricing language outside pricing section.
- First screenshot shows value within 5 seconds.
- Feature bullets avoid guarantees.
- No beta language remains on public pages.
- App is full visibility when ready for organic discovery.

## Review strategy
Ask for honest feedback only after a real user completes a useful scan. Do not ask for a positive review and do not offer incentives.
