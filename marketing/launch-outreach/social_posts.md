# Social launch posts

## LinkedIn launch post

Margin Sentinel is now live on the Shopify App Store.

It is a lightweight margin guardrail for Shopify merchants who want to catch SKU-level margin leaks before they become expensive.

What it checks:
- losing products
- low-margin variants
- missing cost data
- supplier CSV costs
- inventory value exposed to margin gaps
- suggested minimum margin guidance

The app is read-only. It does not change prices. It helps merchants see what needs attention first.

App Store listing:
https://apps.shopify.com/margin-sentinel

I am looking for the first merchants, operators, and Shopify agencies willing to try it and give blunt feedback.
