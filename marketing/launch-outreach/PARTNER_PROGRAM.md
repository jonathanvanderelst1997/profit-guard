# Agency and consultant partner program

## Offer
Margin Sentinel can be used as a quick margin diagnostic during Shopify audits, migrations, CRO work, pricing reviews, supplier cost changes, or catalog cleanup projects.

## Partner pitch
Use Margin Sentinel to give clients a practical SKU-level margin check before pricing, supplier, or promotion decisions.

## Why agencies care
- Helps uncover obvious catalog issues quickly.
- Gives a concrete fix list clients understand.
- Adds value to audits and retainers.
- Does not require a heavy BI or accounting implementation.
- Read-only app reduces operational risk.
