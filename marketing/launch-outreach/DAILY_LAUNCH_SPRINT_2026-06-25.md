# Margin Sentinel 7-day launch sprint

Window: 2026-06-25 to 2026-07-02.

Goal: create visible daily progress toward the first paying merchants without burning sender reputation or community trust.

## Non-negotiable rules

- Every day must produce one concrete artifact or action.
- Do not send bulk outreach.
- Do not post public replies or send contact forms without exact approval for that specific message and destination.
- One good, relevant message is better than twenty vague messages.
- Track every send, reply, bounce, hidden post, and follow-up date.
- Use the App Store listing as the main conversion page: `https://apps.shopify.com/margin-sentinel`.

## Daily operating rhythm

1. Check replies and engagement.
   - Outlook recent messages.
   - LinkedIn comments or DMs if accessible.
   - Shopify Community moderation status.
   - App Store installs or obvious support messages.

2. Run the local brief.
   - Command: `npm run marketing:brief`
   - Purpose: see due follow-ups, pending drafts, and next seven days.

3. Do one concrete acquisition action.
   - Prepare 3-5 merchant leads.
   - Draft one specific outreach message.
   - Prepare one no-link community reply.
   - Improve one SEO/resource page.
   - Prepare one public post or short demo asset.
   - Ask Jonathan for exact approval when an external send/post is ready.

4. Log the result.
   - Update `marketing/launch-outreach/outreach_tracker.md`.
   - Add a dated send log when anything is sent or submitted.

## What success looks like this week

- 4-6 very targeted merchant/agency messages sent, not a giant cold blast.
- 10-20 qualified leads researched and ranked.
- 2-3 community-safe answers drafted.
- 1-2 SEO/resource improvements prepared.
- A clear answer on whether agencies or direct merchants are responding better.

## What to avoid

- Posting app links into old threads without context.
- Re-sending the Shopify Community post that was hidden.
- Mass-emailing scraped contacts.
- Claiming we know a store has bad margins from public data.
- Overpromising automation. Margin Sentinel is read-only and does not change prices automatically.
