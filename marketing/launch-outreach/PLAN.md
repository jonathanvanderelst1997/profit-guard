# Margin Sentinel launch outreach plan

## Goal
Get the first 10 real installs, 3 discovery calls, and 1 public review for Margin Sentinel.

## Positioning
Margin Sentinel helps Shopify merchants find SKU-level margin leaks before they hide inside wider accounting reports. It scans product/variant selling prices, Shopify unit costs, and supplier CSV costs, then shows losing products, low-margin variants, missing supplier data, inventory risk, and suggested minimum margin guidance.

## Target buyers
1. Shopify merchants with 100+ SKUs.
2. Fashion, beauty, lifestyle, outdoor, electronics accessories, parts, gifts, and home goods stores.
3. Shopify agencies that manage optimization, CRO, operations, or migrations for merchants.
4. Shopify consultants and fractional ecommerce operators.

## Why agencies first
Agencies can introduce Margin Sentinel to multiple merchants. The pitch is not "replace your analytics stack"; it is "use this as a lightweight SKU margin guardrail before pricing and supplier data problems become expensive."

## Outreach rules
- Send only targeted, relevant emails.
- Personalize the first sentence.
- Use one clear ask: "Worth a quick look for your Shopify clients?"
- Include the Shopify App Store URL.
- Include a simple opt-out line.
- Track every contact and avoid repeated messages.
- Do not scrape private personal emails. Use public business/contact emails or contact forms.

## 14-day launch rhythm

### Day 1
- Create first 5 agency email drafts.
- Post launch announcement on LinkedIn/X.
- Ask 5 friends/operators for installs or feedback.

### Day 2
- Send 5 agency emails after review.
- Find 20 Shopify merchants in SKU-heavy niches.
- Create 5 merchant-specific drafts.

### Day 3
- Post short product demo GIF/video.
- DM 10 Shopify consultants on LinkedIn with a softer partnership angle.

### Day 4
- Follow up with agencies that opened/replied, if any.
- Add 10 app ecosystem contacts: Klaviyo, inventory, ERP, and Shopify implementation consultants.

### Day 5
- Publish a short article: "3 margin leaks Shopify merchants miss until it is too late."
- Link to the app listing.

### Day 7
- First follow-up email to non-responders.
- Ask first users for a review after they run a scan.

### Day 10
- Share a mini case study from the dev store/demo data.
- Offer a free 15-minute margin leak audit for first 10 merchants.

### Day 14
- Review metrics:
  - Emails sent
  - Replies
  - Installs
  - Trials started
  - First scans
  - Reviews requested

## Current priority
Get 5 agency conversations, not vanity traffic.
