- Visibility: Indexed in the public Analytics category.
- Listing URL: https://apps.shopify.com/margin-sentinel
- Price: $15/month with 14-day trial.
- Review count: 0.
- Rough Analytics category position checked 2026-07-02: page 45, position 3, approximately rank 1,059.
- Production metrics checked 2026-07-02: 128 public page views, 3 app opens, 1 scan started, 1 scan completed, 0 billing approval requests, 0 active subscriptions.

## North star for the first 30 days
Get 10 installed stores, 5 completed scans, 3 merchant conversations, and 1 honest review.

## Biggest constraint
The app is visible, but buried. The constraint is not publication anymore; it is trust and ranking signal:

- 0 reviews.
- No confirmed buyer installs.
- No billing approval requests.
- No exports/downloads from real merchants.
- No Built for Shopify badge visible yet.
- Category rank is too deep for organic discovery.

The practical bottleneck is `qualified traffic -> install -> first scan -> proof -> review/trial`.

## Core message
Margin Sentinel is a lightweight Shopify catalog margin guardrail. It helps merchants find SKU-level margin leaks, missing costs, and inventory risk before monthly reports reveal the damage.

## Best early customers
1. Shopify stores with 100+ physical SKUs.
2. Fashion, beauty, accessories, parts, home goods, outdoor, pet, hobby, and gift stores.
3. Stores with supplier CSV price lists.
4. Agencies and consultants who manage pricing, catalog operations, CRO, or ecommerce growth.

## Channel priority

### Tier 1: agency partner outreach
Why: one agency can introduce multiple merchants.
Action: send 5-10 highly personalized emails per week, then follow up once.

### Tier 2: merchant founder/operator outreach
Why: fast direct learning and installs.
Action: target stores with clear SKU/catalog complexity and public business contact routes.

### Tier 3: founder social content
Why: builds trust and proof over time.
Action: 3 LinkedIn posts per week, 3 short X posts per week.

### Tier 4: content and SEO
Why: compounds around exact pain points.
Action: publish practical posts about missing product costs, supplier increases, SKU margin leaks, and Shopify unit cost hygiene.

### Tier 5: communities
Why: useful if value-first, risky if spammy.
Action: answer real Shopify Community/Reddit questions; mention Margin Sentinel only when it directly solves the issue.

### Tier 6: paid App Store ads
Why: Shopify says App Store ads can place apps in search results, category pages, and the homepage. This can create install velocity, but it gets expensive quickly if activation is weak.
Action: start only with a tiny research campaign after tracking is verified and the first-scan proof page/demo is ready.

## Revised 30-day ranking strategy - 2026-07-02

### Principle
Do not compete as a broad profit dashboard. TrueProfit, BeProfit, Lifetimely, and Profitario already own broad net-profit analytics with far more reviews. Margin Sentinel should own the narrow problem:

> "Find missing costs and loss-making SKUs before discounts, ads, or supplier changes eat margin."

### Week 1: proof and tracking
- Keep daily outreach to 5-10 highly targeted merchants/agencies.
- Turn every reply into a founder-led free 20-SKU margin leak audit.
- Add or promote one public proof artifact using safe demo/sample data.
- Verify GA4/Meta listing tracking daily:
  - App Store listing views.
  - Add App/AddToCart.
  - App opens.
  - First scan completed.
  - Trial/billing approval requested.
- Do not judge demand from installs alone; judge first-scan completion and replies.

### Week 2: first reviews and community
- Ask for honest App Store reviews only after useful scan completion.
- Publish one value-first community answer per day only where the problem is directly relevant.
- Use the resource pages that already exist as non-sales links.
- Capture objections from replies and update listing copy weekly.

### Week 3: controlled paid test
- If tracking and first-scan activation are clean, run Shopify App Store ads at $5-$15/day for 7 days.
- Start with narrow exact/broad-match research terms:
  - `missing costs`
  - `COGS tracker`
  - `profit margin`
  - `SKU margin`
  - `supplier costs`
  - `low margin products`
  - `discount margin`
- Add negative keywords from irrelevant search terms.
- Pause if there are 100+ qualified clicks with 0 installs or if installs do not complete a first scan.

### Week 4: ranking review
- Re-check Analytics category page and target keywords.
- Compare:
  - category position
  - Meta/GA4 listing events
  - app opens
  - first scans
  - exports/imports
  - billing approval requests
  - reviews
- Keep the channel that produces first scans, not just clicks.

Updated 2026-07-02: run the ad test only after a proof/demo path is ready and tracking shows App Store clicks cleanly.

## What not to do
- Do not mass-send generic emails.
- Do not offer incentives for positive reviews.
- Do not claim guaranteed profit improvement.
- Do not spam Shopify Community or Reddit with launch links.
- Do not run paid ads before the onboarding and billing flow are stable.

## First commercial offer
"Install Margin Sentinel, run your first scan, and send us the top finding. We will help interpret the first result and suggest what to check next."

## Follow-up metric targets
- Cold agency reply rate: 5-10%.
- Merchant reply rate: 3-7%.
- Trial-to-first-scan: 60%+.
- First-scan-to-paid retention: unknown; learn manually.
- First review: after a merchant has actually completed a scan and found value.
