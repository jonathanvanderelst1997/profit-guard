# Review and retention plan

## Review rules
- Ask for honest reviews only after a merchant has used the app.
- Do not create fake reviews.
- Do not offer discounts, gifts, money, or other incentives for positive reviews.
- Do not pressure users to leave only positive feedback.

## When to ask
Ask after one of these moments:
- The merchant completes a scan.
- The merchant exports findings.
- The merchant imports supplier costs successfully.
- The merchant replies that Margin Sentinel found something useful.

## Review request email
Subject: quick favor if Margin Sentinel was useful

Hi {name},

Thanks again for trying Margin Sentinel.

If the scan helped you find something useful, would you be open to leaving an honest Shopify App Store review?

Listing:
https://apps.shopify.com/margin-sentinel

No pressure either way. Even short feedback helps other merchants understand whether the app is worth testing.

Best,
Jonathan

## Retention check-in email
Subject: did the first margin scan catch anything useful?

Hi {name},

Quick check-in: did the first Margin Sentinel scan catch anything useful, confusing, or wrong?

The feedback I am most interested in:
1. Were the findings understandable?
2. Was the "fix first" recommendation useful?
3. Did supplier cost import fit your workflow?
4. What would make this worth keeping every month?

Best,
Jonathan
